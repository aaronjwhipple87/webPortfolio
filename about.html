Project Process

1 Discuss and Plan

Tell me all about your new or existing project along with what your expectations are etc.

2 Research and Ideas

Once we are all on the same page I'll research similar projects and competition to relay to you with new ideas to separate your company.

3 Design and Approval

After a more defined idea of what you are looking for I will create several mockups, beginning with low-fidelity all the way to high-fidelity mockups seeking approval along each step.

4 Build and Launch

Once the final high-fidelity mockup is approved I will build and launch your project and finalize any further adjustments you may want.
